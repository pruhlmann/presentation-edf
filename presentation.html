1. Flow Matching Source Distribution

Need a better source than Gaussian

2. Simulation-Informed Source

Designed \(\pi(\theta | y)\) to be closer to target posterior \(p(\theta|y)\)

3. Our Source Form

We chose a source of the form:

$$ \pi(\theta |y) = \int p(\theta|x)q(x|y)dx $$

Learn \(q(x|y)\) with \((S(\theta_i),y_i)\)

Joint optimization for more robustness
